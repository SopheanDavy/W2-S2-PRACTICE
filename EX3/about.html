Our Mission and Values

Founded in 2023, our company started with a simple idea: design should be accessible to everyone. We are a small, passionate team dedicated to developing powerful, user-friendly tools that unlock creativity. We believe in open-source principles and constantly iterate based on user feedback to create the best possible experience.

  • Innovation: Always seeking new ways to simplify complex tasks.
  • Accessibility: Providing tools that anyone, anywhere can use.
  • Community: Building a supportive network of users and creators.
  • Integrity: Maintaining transparency and trust in all our practices.
